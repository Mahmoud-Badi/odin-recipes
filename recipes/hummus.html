Hummus Recipe

Home

Description

This creamy and smooth hummus is perfect as a dip or spread. It's made with chickpeas, tahini, garlic, and olive oil, bringing a savory and nutty flavor.

Ingredients

  • Chickpeas (garbanzo beans)
  • Tahini
  • Olive oil
  • Garlic
  • Lemon juice
  • Ground cumin
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Blend the chickpeas, tahini, garlic, lemon juice, cumin, and salt in a food processor until smooth.
  2. While blending, slowly add olive oil to reach your desired consistency.
  3. Serve the hummus in a bowl, drizzle with extra olive oil, and enjoy with pita bread or veggies!
