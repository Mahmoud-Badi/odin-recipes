Lasagna Recipe

Home

Description

This classic Italian lasagna is rich, meaty, and full of cheesy goodness.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions.
  3. Brown the ground beef and mix with tomato sauce.
  4. Layer the noodles, ricotta, beef sauce, and mozzarella.
  5. Bake for 45 minutes and let cool before serving.
