Chicken Alfredo Recipe

Home

Description

This creamy and flavorful Chicken Alfredo is a delicious and satisfying dish that's easy to make.

Ingredients

  • Chicken breast
  • Fettuccine pasta
  • Heavy cream
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Garlic
  • Butter
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook fettuccine pasta according to the package instructions.
  2. Season and cook chicken breasts in a pan with olive oil until browned and cooked through.
  3. Remove chicken and make the Alfredo sauce by melting butter and adding garlic, cream, and cheese.
  4. Slice the chicken and add to the sauce, mixing with pasta.
  5. Serve and enjoy the creamy, cheesy goodness!
